SAMBA

Indoor Environmental Quality (IEQ) is the environmental quality of a building, and is important to occupant comfort, health and productivity. SAMBA is an IEQ monitoring system developed by Tom Parkinson. This small and inconspicuous device - pictured right - offers continuous monitoring of a range of IEQ factors across the day, and is relatively cheap.

The SAMBA device works by taking measurements of its environment at multiple intervals in a day. Multiple SAMBAs from a floor in a building will combine their data, which is then sent to a third party device where it is uploaded to a cloud. This allows you to monitor data to see if your environmental quality is up to standard for maximised efficiency.

SAMBAmate the SAMBA companion app

SAMBAmate is a companion app for your SAMBA device/s. With the SAMBAmate onboarding web app, help your staff understand the importance of IEQ, and how SAMBA can be utilised to identify IEQ issues in your workplace and improve them.
