SAMBA

SAMBA is an IEQ (Indoor Environmental Quality) monitoring system developed by Tom Parkinson. His small and inconspicuous device offers continuous monitoring of a range of IEQ factors across the day, and is relatively cheap.

The SAMBA device works by taking measurements of its environment at multiple intervals in a day. Multiple SAMBAs from a floor in a building will combine their data, which is then sent to a third party device where it is uploaded to a cloud. This is finally outputted as an easy to operate dashboard, so you can see if your environmental quality is up to standard for maximised productivity.
